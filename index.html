Introduction

Government Islamia Science college Sukkur is one of the biggest and the most representative educational institute of upper Sindh.The institutions has a very prestigious record of its previous achievements in the educated society.This College was established in 1956 and shifted to the new building in 1962 and was nationalized in september 1972.The College was bifurcated into two independent and seprate colleges in 1973 having seprate establishment,teaching and administrative staff as Govt.Islamia College Sukkur and Govt.Islamia Arts/Commerce College Sukkur.
Government Islamia Science College Sukkur consists of intermediate,Degree Section and BS(CS)four year programme.
Since nationalization,this institution has achieved enviable thriumphs both in the co-curricular fields. It stands grouped among the five most prestigiuos college of Sindh.
